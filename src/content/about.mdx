## Why this exists

This site is being rebuilt in two passes. The current pass focuses on structure,
typography, and route completeness before any real content migration happens.

## What is real already

- The frontsite information architecture
- The layout rhythm between light and dark sections
- The shared MDX rendering path
- The local content model for articles, projects, and services

## What is intentionally temporary

- Most copy
- Most cover images
- All stats
- Contact details

The goal is to keep the shell clean enough that a later content pass only
replaces files inside `src/content/` rather than rewriting page components.
